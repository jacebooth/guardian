The Traveler is one of the most mysterious and central entities in the Destiny universe. It is a colossal, spherical being of unknown origin that serves as the source of the Light — a cosmic force of creation, life, and hope. The Traveler’s arrival on planets has historically led to periods of extraordinary growth and technological advancement, known as Golden Ages.
